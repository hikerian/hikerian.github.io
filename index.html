Intuitive photo indexing app PhotoIndexer

Having trouble finding your BitLocker recovery key, serial number of your electronics, or photos you took for business purposes?
PhotoIndexer solves the hassle of creating and classifying albums for one photo, but wasting time searching for the photos you need among the many photos stored on your phone.
PhotoIndexer allows you to index and manage photos in the Photos app or photos taken with the camera in various forms.
Even if you don't use it often, it provides the features you need when you need it.

Feature

Registration

You can take a new photo or index a photo already stored in the Photos app

Indexing

You can register by adding a description and tag to the photos that are not enough just by categorizing the album

Easy Search

You can search registered photos by sorting them by description, tag, location where the photo was taken, and the date of registration

Sharing

You can share the photos you find with other apps

How It Works

Add descriptions and tags to your photos

You can search registered photos by date, tag, and location

Registered photos can be searched using period and search terms

Select a photo to view detailed information about it

Selected photos can be shared with other apps
